FAQ

Q: What’s the best time to visit Taniti?
A: April–August is sunny and perfect for a vacation destination.

Q: Do I need a visa?
A: US citizens do not need a visa for stays under 30 days.

Q: Is the water safe to drink?
A: Absolutely! Our tap water is purified via triple osmosis and incredibly delicious.
